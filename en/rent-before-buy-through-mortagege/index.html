Loyagami helps individuals to own beautiful homes while building a wealth of personal property assets

What are the property requirements that can participate in this program?

The conditions are that the housing has been completed, is ready to use and preferably one that has a certificate of ownership

Who can join this program?

Every individual who is over 21 years of age and over and has a fixed income, whether as an employee, entrepreneur or professional, we do not limit that individuals must work and have a salary slip. We apply fairly strict standards with the aim of creating a comfortable, safe and healthy community of residents

If I already have a house candidate and agent, can Loyagami help?

We prefer developers or property asset owners who have collaborated with us before, because we really maintain the quality of the property assets that we will buy for you by looking at development and maintenance audit records that are at least 3 years.

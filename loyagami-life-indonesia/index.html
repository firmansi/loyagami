The Benefits

Bagaimana kami dapat membantu anda?

Layanan  yang  membantu pengembangan dan perlindungan bisnis serta layanan penunjang bagi kesejahteraan pelaku bisnis dan pekerja yang dibantu oleh asisten robotik melalui dialog atau aplikasi berbasis percakapan

Pengembangan dan perlindungan bisnis yang diberikan mencakup
  • Keberlangsungan bisnis melalui program alihdaya bisnis dan opsi pendanaan ventura
  • Perlindungan tempat kerja dan usaha dari resiko musibah, kecelakaan kerja dan kejahatan
  • Layanan penunjang bagi kesejahteraan pelaku bisnis dan pekerja berupa perlindungan serta pengelolaan hunian dan berbagai program untuk menjaga produktifitas serta kreatifitas kerja

Kami menawarkan beragam penawaran melalui keanggotaan kami yang bertujuan untuk menyeimbangkan hidup Anda

  • Recreation

    Get specialty tour . travel experiences from our local and overseas partners including single or multi pass from Loyagami to stay at leading boutique hotels around the world

  • Improve

    Business development programs for various digital economy and services industry

  • Nourish

    Get a variety of daily, specialized and diet catering programs and dining voucher offerings at Loyagami's various affiliate restaurants.

  • Contribute

    Help those who are in need of help in community and get Loyagami Life Point that can be redeemed with cash money or other gift vouchers

  • Connect

    Get ideas and business opportunities by working individually or in groups in a variety of co-working space that can only be accessed through single and multi pass

  • Grocery

    Meet your daily needs through a partnership network that not only gets you special prices but also helps others

  • Care

    Get the care you need from dedicated health, nutrition, and family care providers through Loyagami Life for you, family, kids or parents
